Final Project

Asteroid Dodge

Welcome to my final CSC 106 programming project page. Here, I am embedding a project that I have worked on for a while with my partner, John Luzzi.
The program is an asteroid dodge game. The game has an information screen to explain how to play.
You press the left and right arrow keys to move diagonally up and to the left, and diagonally down and to the right.
You are the rocketship character, and the object of the game is to dodge as many asteroids as possible.
Your score will go up as time goes on, however if you get hit by an asteroid you will lose 5 points. The highest possible score is 48 points.
Try to earn a high score! When all 100 asteroids have passed, the game will end.
